Awards & Recognition

Service Desk – Click Me

Meezan Bank is Pakistan’s premier Islamic bank, operating with more than 980 branches in over 320 cities across the country. The Bank has been recognized by various local and international institutes on several occasions for its phenomenal year-on-year growth and contribution to the domain of Islamic finance in the region. Meezan Bank has been acknowledged as the Best Bank of Pakistan for the third time at the Pakistan Banking Awards - 2023, the most prestigious awards in the country’s financial sector. The award ceremony is held by the Institute of Bankers Pakistan, in partnership with the Dawn Media Group and in collaboration with A.F. Ferguson and Company. The judging panel, consisting of highly respected luminaries with substantial experience in the financial sector, was chaired by Mr. Syed Salim Raza, Former Governor of the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP). The five-member jury based its decision on the banks' performance, affirming our commitment to Islamic banking and our consistent delivery of higher standards of customer service quality to further elevate service standards. The Bank was previously recognized at the same platform as the Best Bank for the years 2018 and 2020. With a journey that is just 25 years old, Meezan Bank is both humbled and delighted to receive this award. We express our gratitude to all our customers for their continued patronage and trust in Islamic banking and Meezan Bank!
